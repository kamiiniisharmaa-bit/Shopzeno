1. Information We Collect

Shopzeno collects information to provide better services and a seamless shopping experience. The categories include:

CategoryExamplesLegal Basis (where applicable)

Payment DataBilling address, payment card details (processed via PCI-DSS partners)Contract performance

We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, genetic, biometric) unless you voluntarily provide it for specific product reviews.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us personalize your experience, process orders, and improve Shopzeno. Specific uses include:

  • Order fulfillment: Processing payments, shipping, and sending order confirmations.
  • Customer support: Responding to inquiries, returns, and warranty claims.
  • Marketing & personalization: Sending promotions (with opt-out) and recommending products based on your interests.
  • Site improvement: Analyzing trends, monitoring security, and enhancing user interface.
  • Legal compliance: Fraud detection, tax calculations, and responding to lawful requests.

3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

Shopzeno respects your privacy and only shares data with trusted partners under strict confidentiality:

  • Service providers: Shipping carriers (e.g., FedEx, USPS), payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), and IT support.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights of Shopzeno.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with notice.

We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Shopzeno uses cookies, web beacons, and local storage to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize ads. Types include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality (cart, checkout).
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics, Hotjar to improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Retargeting campaigns via social platforms.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site features.

🍪 Cookie Manager: You can reset consent anytime via our Cookie Preferences link in footer.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., California, EU, UK), you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten): Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to object: Opt-out of direct marketing and certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@shopzeno.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

Shopzeno implements industry-standard measures including SSL encryption, firewalls, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available.

7. Children's Privacy

Shopzeno does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately. We will delete such information promptly.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records), and resolve disputes. Typically, transaction data is kept for 7 years, while marketing preferences are retained until you opt out.

9. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website may contain links to external sites (e.g., social media, brand partners). Shopzeno is not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. We encourage you to read their policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be notified via email or a banner on our website. Your continued use of Shopzeno after changes signifies acceptance.
